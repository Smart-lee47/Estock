Our Services

From storage to full logistics management — Stockish provides everything your business needs to store, track, and move goods efficiently.

🏢

Warehousing

Our core service — rent dedicated storage space inside any of our 5 purpose-built warehouses. Whether you need a few shelves or an entire section, we offer flexible slot-based rental plans tailored to businesses of all sizes. You choose the warehouse that fits your goods, and we handle the rest.

  • Dedicated storage slots in your chosen warehouse
  • Flexible rental duration — weekly, monthly, or long-term
  • Organized shelving and labeled sections
  • Easy access during operating hours
  • Compatible with all warehouse types: General, Cold, Bulk, Secure, Logistics
❄️

Cold Chain Management

For businesses dealing in perishable or temperature-sensitive goods, our Cold Chain service provides end-to-end refrigeration management. From the moment your goods arrive at Warehouse B to the point of dispatch, we maintain the required temperature range and log every change for compliance and quality assurance.

  • Dedicated storage in Warehouse B (Cold Storage)
  • Maintained temperature range of 2°C to 8°C
  • 24/7 automated temperature monitoring
  • Backup cooling systems in case of power failure
  • Suitable for food products, medicine, dairy, and beverages
🚛

Logistics & Distribution

Move your goods faster. Our logistics service connects your storage directly to your delivery operations. Based out of Warehouse E (our Logistics Hub), we coordinate pickups, handle packaging, and manage outbound distribution — so your products reach their destination on time, every time.

  • Inbound and outbound delivery coordination
  • 8 dedicated loading bays at Warehouse E
  • Packaging and labeling support
  • Same-day dispatch for registered clients
  • Ideal for e-commerce businesses and distributors
📦

Bulk Storage

Designed for businesses that deal in large volumes or heavy goods. Warehouse C provides high-ceiling storage, reinforced flooring, and full forklift access — making it the right choice for construction companies, manufacturers, and importers with oversized or heavy inventory needs.

  • High-ceiling storage — suitable for tall or stacked goods
  • Reinforced flooring for heavy equipment and materials
  • Full forklift and pallet truck access
  • 2,400 m² of floor space in Warehouse C
  • Open Mon–Sat from 6am to 9pm
